Tree-Jacked: Who are we?

We are a small group from Death Valley. We love wood, and anything that lets us use are sharp, gas-powered, instruments. We are known for our ability to take wood away, make it beautiful, and sell it to artsy people for the big bucks. We thrive in enviroments where there are trees. We will do anything for them or to them. We might even start a reforrestation program if we get paid for it.
